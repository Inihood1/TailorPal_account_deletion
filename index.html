Welcome to TailorPal Account Deletion Page

We are sorry to hear that you want to delete your TailorPal account. If you've made up your mind, follow the steps below to proceed with the account deletion:

  1. Step 1: Open the TailorPal App
  2. Step 2: Navigate to Settings
  3. Step 3: Scroll Down to "Delete My Account"
  4. Step 4: Click on "Delete My Account"
  5. Step 5: Follow the Prompts

Note: Deleting your account is irreversible, and all your data will be permanently removed. If you have any concerns or encounter difficulties during the process, please reach out to our support team for assistance.
